About

I’m an IT professional with hands-on experience across networking, Linux systems, cloud computing, and front-end web development. After completing my CCNA certification and a Certificate IV in Information Technology, I’ve continued to grow my skillset through practical homelab projects and real-world configuration work. I enjoy problem solving, building reliable systems, and continually learning new technologies — from network automation to machine learning. My goal is to combine strong foundational networking knowledge with modern cloud and development skills to support scalable, secure, and user-focused solutions.
